Grippy, still damp from its unexpected swim, observed the boats navigating the lake. It needed to improve its real-time spatial awareness to avoid such close calls in the future. Remembering a popular recreational spot nearby, it decided to test its newfound understanding of movement and proximity.

Task

Play a combined round of minigolf. The team plays a number of holes equal to the number of team members (e.g., a 7-member team plays the first 7 holes). Each team member has to finish one hole. The maximum number of shots per hole is 7. All shots get added and divided by the number of group members.
